Employment Opportunities

Employment

We currently have no available employment opportunities with the club. However, we periodically have a need for coaches and coxswains, especially during summers. To be notified when future opportunities are available, please contact club president Landon Bartley at landon@grrowing.org.

Volunteers

Volunteers are always needed. If you are interested in volunteering as a coach, at a regatta, or in any other capacity, please contact club president Landon Bartley at landon@grrowing.org. We can definitely find something for you to do! (Members, please log in and check out the stewardship page.)

Opportunities in the Area

The crew programs for both Forest Hills Northern and Forest Hills Eastern High School are seeking applications for their respective Head Coaching Positions. Interested candidates can apply online by going to FHPS.net, selecting "District" and "Employment". Questions can be directed to Athletic Director Cory Anderson at coanderson@fhps.net.
