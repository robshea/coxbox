School Eligibility

Grand Rapids Junior Rowing is a high school level rowing team that is mostly comprised of students from schools that do not currently have their own rowing program. We also welcome students from schools which do have a rowing program, however, students who choose to compete with us while their school offers a program can not return to their program that season.

Area High Schools with Rowing Programs

The following area schools have their own scholastic rowing programs. If you attend one of these schools and want to row, please contact that program. Students from one of these schools who choose to compete with us while their team is in season can not return to their team during that season. Grand Rapids Junior Rowing has programs in all four seasons. Some programs below do not have a Summer, Fall or Winter program, and in that case you can row this us while your home team is not active and still have the choice to return to your home program when they start their next active season.

  • East Grand Rapids
  • Forest Hills Central
  • Forest Hills Eastern
  • Forest Hills Northern
  • Lowell
  • Northview
  • Rockford

Per Article III, Section 2. of the Michigan Scholastic Rowing Rules, only students of these schools can participate in their rowing programs. However, students of any other school may row for Grand Rapids Junior Rowing.
