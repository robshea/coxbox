Dues

Season Schedule Cost
Winter January - March $150
Spring April - May $300
Summer June - August $150
Fall September - November $300

Dues cover coaching fees, maintenance & repair of equipment, insurance, entry fees for competitions, and the team’s membership in the U.S. Rowing Association. Dues do not cover uniforms, travel, lodging, and food for regattas.

If you have any questions, contact us.

Waivers

Rowers - please download and print the Annual Waiver, (Adobe Reader required to read file), Click to download (opens in new window) which is required annually (each calendar year) for all rowers. Please fill the form out and bring it to practice to give to your coach.

Payments

You can both apply and pay online, or you can apply online and print an invoice and bring a check to practice with you. Payment plans are available.

Payments are due from all rowers about two weeks after their squad's season starts. Please provide payment no later than the assigned date (varies by season). Rowers who have not paid or set up a payment plan will not be permitted to continue until payment is made.

A very limited number of scholarships may be available, depending upon financial need and availability of funds. Check this form for more details.
