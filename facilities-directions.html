Directions to Boathouse

The Grand Rapids Rowing Association Boathouse is located in Plainfield Township.

291 North Park St NW, Grand Rapids, MI 49544

Important: Use Greenleaf Landscaping entrance to access the boathouse access road. See Google street view of entrance to boathouse access road.

Directions from I-96

  • Exit to US-131 Northbound, follow West River Dr. exit directions below

or

  • Exit to US-131 Southbound, follow Ann St. exit directions below

Directions from I-196

  • Exit at US-131 North
  • Use Ann St. exit or West River Dr. exit See below.

Directions from US-131, West River Dr. exit
  • Exit at West. River Dr. (Exit 91)
  • Turn right (South) on West. River Dr.
  • Turn left (East) on North Park Dr.
  • Enter at Greenleaf Landscaping.

Directions from US-131, Ann St. exit
  • Exit at Ann St. (Exit 88)
  • From northbound 131, turn right (west) on Ann St. (from southbound 131, the street directly off the exit ramp is Turner Ave.)
  • Turn right (North) on Turner Ave.
  • Turn right (North) on West River Dr.
  • Turn right (East) on North Park Dr.
  • Enter at Greenleaf Landscaping.

Ballpark Game Day Traffic

On game days at Fifth Third Ballpark, traffic can back up for quite a distance on US-131 at the West River Dr. exit. We recommend taking the Ann Street exit or find an alternate route.
