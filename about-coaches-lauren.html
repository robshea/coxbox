Lauren Holben

Lauren rowed for Grand Valley State University for four years, in addition to serving on the board her senior year. She participated in such regattas as Head of the Charles in Boston, Dad Vail in Philadelphia, and she finished her collegiate rowing career overseas at the Henley Women's Regatta in Henley-Upon-Thames, England in June of 2010.

She received a gold medal in the Varsity Women's 4+ at ACRA National Championships, as well as silver in the Women's DII/DIII 8+ at Dad Vail her Junior year and 4th place at Head of the Charles in the Women's Collegiate 8+ her sophomore year. She also helped coach the high school summer program at GRRA, and taught sculling classes at the Grand Valley boathouse.

Lauren currently lives in Grand Rapids with her dog Emma and coaches Adult Learn-to-Row classes as well as the masters competitive program for the GRRA. She also coaches Rockford High School's JV and Novice rowing teams and is CPR and USRowing Level II certified.
