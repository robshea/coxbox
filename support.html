Volunteer

One big way is by volunteering to help us out around the boathouse or at regattas. We ask all members to help out as part of their membership; but anyone is welcome to keep the club moving along! Please contact us for volunteer opportunities (members, sign in and check the "Stewardship" page).

Give a Gift of Rowing

Maybe you decided that rowing's just not for you (yet!), but you know that your family, friends, coworkers, or acquaintances would really enjoy it! Purchase a gift certificate from the GRRA, which can be used for Learn to Row, Learn to Scull, or Indoor Rowing classes; memberships; private coaching, and more! A GRRA gift certificate makes a great gift!

Donate

The Grand Rapids Rowing Association is a 501(c)(3) charitable nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible in accordance with IRS regulations. Our mission is to provide community wellness through rowing and other river sports, and our charitable community programs rely on donations to keep going. Please consider making a tax-deductible donation. Thank you in advance!
