Our 100-level Learn-to-Row class are meant for beginners with no prior rowing experience, although it is also a very useful refresher for those who have been off the water for several seasons! Each Learn-to-Row group learns the basics of sweep rowing in eight- and four-person rowing shells. There are also opportunities for friendly competition at the conclusion of each course, at a mini-Regatta coupled with a cookout we call "Boats, Beers, and Burgers". It's a lot of fun and a great way to show off your new rowing skills. Make sure to invite your friends and family... it's the last date of the class!
