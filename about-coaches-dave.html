Dave Morrison

Head Coach, Adult Recreational Rowing

Dave Morrison began his rowing career at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel high school in Wyandotte, MI in the 1970’s. In 1977, he began his coaching career at Mt. Carmel. He then became coach of Ann Arbor’s Huron High School rowing team for 25 seasons. Dave coached Western Michigan University’s club team for 3 years. He has also coached at Ecorse, Wyandotte Boat Club, and Carlson High School. Now based in Kalamazoo, Dave uses his extensive experience to guide the Adult Recreation program.
