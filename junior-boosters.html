GR Crew Boosters

Purpose and Function

The GR Crew Booster Club supports all members and coaches of the GR Crew Novice and Varsity teams. Support may include, but is not limited to, team communications, team travel, team fundraising, team community building & team recruitment. The GR Crew Booster Club is established as a part of the Grand Rapids Rowing Association and will provide monthly reports of all business to the Grand Rapids Rowing Association Board of Directors.

Membership

Membership consists of all parents and guardians of GR Crew novice and varsity team members. Membership shall also be open to any person who subscribes to the purpose and function of the GR Crew Booster Club. Members do not have to have students currently participating in GR Crew to join.

Anyone can join the GR Crew Boosters.

Rights and Responsibilities of GR Crew Booster Members

Families play an integral role in the success and experiences of the GR Crew athletes and in the success of the program overall. The rights and responsibilities of GR Crew Booster members are to:

  • Receive timely GR Crew communications, calendars & initiative information
  • Support GR Crew team events & initiatives
  • Positively represent GR Crew and the GRRA at events and within the larger community
  • Share in community with other GR Crew members & families
  • Contribute to the well being & success of all GR Crew athletes
