Beth Czarnecki

Beth Czarnecki began her rowing career as a freshman on Northview High School's rowing team. There, she rowed for four years, sitting stroke seat all four years. After high school, Beth went on to study at Central Michigan University, however, transferred to Grand Valley State University after one year. She joined the GVSU Rowing Team and rowed for three more years under coaches Lisa Saladino, Dennis Kamrad, and John Bancheri.

Beth was born and raised in Grand Rapids and is currently in her last semester as a Clinical Exercise Science major (Spanish minor) at Grand Valley. She has had experience putting together different exercise programs for various types of athletes in different age groups and of different skill levels. After she receives her undergraduate degree, Beth hopes to continue her education and pursue a Masters in Occupational Therapy.
